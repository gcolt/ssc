PROJECTS THAT WILL NOT BE APPROVED BY ARC Revision: 0                     Date: 08/08/2022

             Description: Original Document

 

 

Purpose: The purpose of this document is to establish guidelines and policies for projects that will not be approved by the ARC.  The ARC is responsible for exterior aesthetics of each lot (see Article 3 for details).

 

Project List

1)    Accordion, Bahama, or Colonial hurricane shutters that can be seen from the street

2)    Outdoor shed/detached garage

3)    Screen door for the garage

4)    Screen for the front porch

5)    Anything other than a screen to enclose a pool/lanai/patio

6)    Metal/tile roof

7)    Wooden/chain link fence

8)    Permanent sign

9)    Permanent basketball goal

10) Improvements that do not conform to approved color schemes

11) Improvements (fences/hedges/walls) that negatively impact aesthetics

12) Improvements that negatively affect water drainage or utility easements

13) Improvements that are not located on the lot owner’s property

 

If you have any questions, please contact the property manager.
