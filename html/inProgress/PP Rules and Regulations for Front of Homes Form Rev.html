PINNACLE OF PORT ORANGE

HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION, INC.

 

Rules & Regulations for the Front Area of our Homes

 

Revision: 1                   Date: 03/06/2024

             Description: Original Document

 

 

Purpose: The purpose of this document is to establish guidelines, rules and regulations for homeowners, in addition to Article 3 in the CCR’s, Restrictions and Restrictive Covenants.

 

Homeowners are responsible for the upkeep of the following:

 

  1. Sidewalks and Driveway aprons to be kept clean and pressure wash.
  2. Driveways are to be kept clean and pressure wash.
  3. Trim dangling Oak Tree branches that are located over the sidewalk.
  4. Maintain grass from sidewalk to the road (fertilize, trim, edge & mow)
  5. Maintain sprinkler system from the sidewalk to the road.
  6. Kill weeds that might be growing in your driveway, sidewalks or landscape beds.
  7. Remove any dead palm fronds from your trees.
  8. White Vinyl Fencing should be kept cleaned and pressure washed.
  9. Ensure legible house numbers that can be seen from the street.
  10. No modifications to the house exterior, including street scape, without ARC approval.
  11. No parking across the sidewalk, this blocks pedestrian access to the sidewalk and is against the City of Port Orange Ordinance
  12. Trash and Recyclables are not to be put out more than 12 hours before each pick up day.
  13. All Bushes and shrubs (not palm or Oaks) must be 2 feet from the soffit.  All leggy shoots 12’ or greater must be trimmed.
