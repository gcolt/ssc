PROJECTS THAT DO NOT REQUIRE AN ARC APPLICATION Revision: 0               Date: 08/08/2022

             Description: Original Document

 

 

Purpose: The purpose of this document is to establish guidelines and policies for projects that do not require an application since the ARC is responsible for exterior aesthetics of each lot (see Article 3 for details).

 

Project List

1)    Interior remodeling

2)    Repainting/replacing in accordance with the originally approved plans/specifications

a)     House exterior walls/trim

b)    Front door

c)     Garage door

d)    White gutters

e)     White fascia

f)     Patio

3)    Replacing screens on an enclosure

4)    Minor roof shingle replacement

5)    Relining a pool

6)    Replacing an air conditioner/heat pump

7)    Replacing sod

8)    Replacing trees/hedges/landscaping

 

If you have any questions, please contact the property manager.
