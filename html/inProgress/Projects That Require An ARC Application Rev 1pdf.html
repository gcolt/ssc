PINNACLE OF PORT ORANGE 

HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION, INC.

 

PROJECTS THAT REQUIRE AN ARC APPLICATION

                     Revision: 1                   Date: 09/06/2022

             Description: Added Items 2o – 2r

 

 

Purpose: The purpose of this document is to establish guidelines and policies for projects that require an application since the ARC is responsible for reviewing modifications that affect the exterior aesthetics of each lot (see Article 3 for details).  Note that installation of new items may require a permit from the City of Port Orange.

 

Project List

1)    Repainting items with a new color or replacing items with a new type/color

a)    House exterior walls/trim

b)    Front door

c)    Garage door

d)    Gutters (other than white)

e)    Fascia (other than white)

f)     Curbing and lawn edging

g)    Patio/screened enclosure

h)    Fencing

i)     Driveway/driveway pavers

j)     Windows

k)    Roof replacement

2)    Installation of new items

a)    Gutters (other than white)

b)    Curbing and lawn edging

c)    Patio/screened Enclosure

d)    Fencing

e)    Driveway pavers

f)     Hurricane shutters

g)    Solar panels

h)    Swimming pool/hot tub/jacuzzi

i)     Sliding glass door

j)     Fire pit (permanent)

k)    Flag pole/light pole (self-standing)

l)     Screen/storm door on the front of the house

m)  Driveway coatings up to the sidewalk

n)    Satellite TV dish or antenna

o)    Stacked curbing (must be bonded together)

p)    Propane tank (in-ground or above ground)

q)    Whole-house generator

r)     Secondary HVAC
